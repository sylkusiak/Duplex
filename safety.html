Your safety is our top priority.

Thank you for choosing to dine here with us at The Duplex. To help ensure the safety of our guests and staff during this time we have put in place a new dining standard.

After each reservation, we will sanitize the table, chairs and the surrounding area. Similarly, we will do the same for restroom service.

Party sizes are limited to 10ppl per table and will have a reservation limit time of 2 hours during the week and 90 minutes on the weekends for brunch. We ask that you respect this window of time as it allows us time to properly clean.

Please BYOM (bring your own mask) Everyone must wear a face mask indoors covering their nose and mouth, except while eating and drinking (unless the state removes the mask mandate for public businesses). All staff members will be required to wear a mask.

We have implemented an Order & Pay system which gives our guests complete control on when they order and how often they order from the convenience of their phone.

We ask if you're experiencing any symptoms consistent with Covid-19 or if someone has been in contact with someone exposed to Covid-19 please stay at home and enjoy our takeout.
